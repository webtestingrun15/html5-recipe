Brownie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetned cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt
  • 4 ounces of chocolate
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 4 large eggs slightly beaten

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Line a 9-by-13-inch baking pan with foil, leaving a 2-inch overhang on at least 2 sides.
  3. Butter the foil.
  4. Whisk together the flour, cocoa powder and salt in a medium bowl.
  5. Melt the butter and chocolate in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring frequently.
  6. Let cool slightly. Stir in the sugar and eggs. Add the flour mixture, and stir to combine.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan, and bake until a toothpick comes out with just a few crumbs, 30 to 35 minutes.
  8. Let the brownies cool completely in the pan on a cooling rack. Use the foil overhang to help lift them out of the pan.
  9. Cut into 16 squares. Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • Two or more large Eggs.
  • 2 cups of Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Oil
  • Your favorite ingredients

Directions:

  1. Mix the eggs milk and butter together in a bowl.
  2. Heat the oil and any other indegredient that you like in large frying pan.
  3. Add the egg mixed earlier to the frying pan and add some salt and pepper.
  4. Cover the pan for five to six minutes or until hardened into paste like color.
  5. Uncover and start stirring.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
